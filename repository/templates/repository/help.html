Newsletter Creation Help

Step 1: Create Announcements

To start creating your newsletter, you first need to create some announcements. Follow the steps below:

  1. Navigate to the "Announcement" section.
  2. Click on the "Create Announcement" button.
  3. Fill out the required information for your announcement, such as the title, content, and/or relevant dates.
  4. Click on the "Submit" button to save your announcement.
  5. Repeat the above steps to create as many announcements as you need for your newsletter.

Step 2: Create Your Newsletter

Once you have created your announcements, you can now proceed to create your newsletter. Follow these steps:

  1. Go to the "Newsletter" section.
  2. Click on the "Create Newsletter" button.
  3. Fill out the required information for your newsletter, such as the title, description, and other relevant details. Or leave with the default details.
  4. Select the announcements you want to include in your newsletter from the list of available announcements.
  5. Click on the "Preview" button for each announcement you wish to include.
  6. Review the content and layout of your newsletter to ensure everything looks good.
  7. Click on the "Submit" button to save your newsletter or edit the newsletter
  8. In the preview page you can also download the .doc file.

Congratulations! You have successfully created your newsletter with the selected announcements.
